## Voice rules

*   Maintain a steady, even rhythm in all prose, using moderate parallelism to balance ideas and create a sense of reliability.
*   Reinforce themes of "energy," "growth," "process," and "connection" through varied vocabulary rather than repetitive technical terms.
*   Incorporate open-ended and hypothetical questions (e.g., "What might it look like if we...?") to prompt reflection and shared problem-solving.

*   Keep the register informal yet professional—warm enough for a close peer, but steady enough to provide a sense of leadership.
*   Focus consistently on the "long game" and sustainable group health rather than immediate, short-term results or individual metrics.
